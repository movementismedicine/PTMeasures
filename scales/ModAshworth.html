Modified Ashworth Scale

Score Description
0 No increase in muscle tone.
1 Slight increase in muscle tone, manifested by a catch and release or minimal resistance at the end of the range of motion.
1+ Slight increase in muscle tone, manifested by a catch followed by minimal resistance through the remainder (less than half) of the range of motion.
2 More marked increase in muscle tone through most of the range of motion, but the affected part is still easily moved.
3 Considerable increase in muscle tone, passive movement is difficult.
4 Affected part(s) are rigid in flexion or extension.
