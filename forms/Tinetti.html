Tinetti Performance-Oriented Mobility Assessment (POMA)

Quantify gait and balance in older adults to track changes over time.

The Tinetti assessment tool is an easily administered task-oriented test that measures an older adult’s gait and balance abilities.

Equipment needed: Hard armless chair, Stopwatch or wristwatch, 15 ft walkway

Completion Time: 10-15 minutes

Scoring: A three-point ordinal scale, ranging from 0-2. “0” indicates the highest level of impairment and “2” the individuals independence. Total Balance Score = 16, Total Gait Score = 12, Total Test Score = 28

Balance Sub-section (Max 16 points)

Initial instructions: Subject is seated in hard, armless chair. The following maneuvers are tested.

Gait Sub-section (Max 12 points)

Initial Instructions: Subject stands with examiner, walks down hallway or across room, first at “usual” pace, then back at “rapid, but safe” pace (using usual walking aids)
