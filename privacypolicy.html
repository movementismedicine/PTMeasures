PTMeasures does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. The app functions entirely as a tool for physical therapy assessments without requiring or collecting any of the following:

PTMeasures is a web-based application that displays standardized physical therapy assessment forms. All calculations and interactions occur locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
